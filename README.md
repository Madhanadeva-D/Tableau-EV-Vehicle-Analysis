# 🚗 Electric Vehicle Data Analysis – Tableau Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

Electric Vehicle Data Analysis project presents a comprehensive and interactive Electric Vehicle (EV) Data Analysis Dashboard built using Tableau.  
The dashboard analyzes EV population data across the United States to uncover adoption trends, manufacturer dominance, state-wise distribution, and the impact of CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility.

The objective is to transform raw EV data into meaningful business insights using KPI-driven analytics and interactive visualizations.

The dashboard includes the following major KPIs:

- **Total PHEV Vehicles:** 33,675

These KPIs provide a high-level summary of the EV ecosystem.

## 📈 Visualizations Included

### 1️⃣ Total Vehicles by Model Year (From 2010 Onwards)
- **Chart Type:** Line / Area Chart  
- **Purpose:** Shows yearly growth in EV adoption.  
- **Insight:** Significant growth observed after 2018, indicating rapid market expansion.

---

### 2️⃣ Total Vehicles by State
- **Chart Type:** Map Visualization  
- **Purpose:** Displays geographical distribution of EVs across U.S. states.  
- **Insight:** Certain states show significantly higher EV penetration, indicating stronger adoption policies and infrastructure.

---

### 3️⃣ Top 10 Total Vehicles by Make
- **Chart Type:** Bar Chart  
- **Purpose:** Identifies leading EV manufacturers.  
- **Insight:** Tesla leads the market by a large margin compared to other brands.

---

### 4️⃣ Total Vehicles by CAFV Eligibility
- **Chart Type:** Pie / Donut Chart  
- **Purpose:** Shows distribution of CAFV Eligible, Not Eligible, and Unknown vehicles.  
- **Insight:** A large portion of EVs qualify for CAFV incentives, highlighting policy influence.

---

### 5️⃣ Top 10 Total Vehicles by Model
- **Chart Type:** Grid View / Table  
- **Purpose:** Highlights most popular EV models.  
- **Insight:** Tesla models dominate the top positions, showing strong brand preference.

## 🔍 Key Insights Derived

- BEVs dominate the EV market with 78% share.
- EV adoption has grown exponentially since 2018.
- Tesla is the clear market leader in both make and model categories.
- EV distribution varies significantly by state.
- Government incentives (CAFV eligibility) positively influence adoption.
